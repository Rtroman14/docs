<Note>Lead Generation is available for Starter plan subscribers and above.</Note>

WebAgent.ai transforms site visits into valuable sales leads. Your AI-powered chatbot engages visitors and captures lead information, effortlessly enriching your sales funnel.

<Steps titleSize="h2">
    <Step title="Enable Lead Capture">
        1. Navigate to your chatbot's settings page.
        2. Locate and enable the **Capture Leads** option.
        3. Select the fields you want to include in your lead capture form.

        <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/images/capture-leads-settings.png" alt="Lead capture settings" />
    </Step>

    <Step title="Form is Sent to User">
        <Info>
            Tip: Offer an incentive (e.g., free resource or discount) to encourage users to submit their contact details.
        </Info>

        The contact form is automatically sent after the first message exchange.

        <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/images/lead-capture-form.png" alt="Lead capture form example" />
    </Step>

    <Step title="Manage Leads">
        1. Access captured leads in the **Leads** tab of your dashboard.
        2. View detailed contact information for each lead.
        3. Analyze conversation history to understand lead interests and needs.

        <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/images/leads-list.png" alt="Leads list in dashboard" />

        <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/images/lead-list-lead-convo.png" alt="Lead conversation history" />
    </Step>

</Steps>

<Info>
    Remember to follow data protection regulations when collecting and storing lead information.
</Info>
