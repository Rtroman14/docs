<Steps titleSize="h2">
    <Step title="Navigate To Website Tools in Squarespace">
        <ol>
            <li>Log in to your Squarespace account and navigate to your website.</li>
            <li>Click on **Website** in the top menu and then select **Pages**.</li>
            <li>Under **Utilities**, click on **Website Tools**.</li>
        </ol>

        <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/images/squarespace-website-tools.png" />
    </Step>
    <Step title="Navigate To Code Injection">
        <ol>
            <li>Under **Website Tools**, click on **Code Injection**.</li>
        </ol>

        <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/images/squarespace-code-injection.png" />
    </Step>
    <Step title="Copy Chatbot Script Tag from WebAgent.ai">
        <ol>
            <li>Log in to your WebAgent.ai account and go to the **Settings**.</li>
            <li>Click on **Embed** and locate the script tag provided.</li>
            <li>Select and copy the entire script tag.</li>
        </ol>

        <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/images/webagent-embed.png" />

    </Step>
    <Step title="Add Script Tag to Header Section">
        <ol>
            <li>Paste the script tag you copied from WebAgent.ai into the **Header** section.</li>
            <li>Click **Save** to save the changes.</li>
        </ol>

        <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/images/squarespace-add-script-tag.png" />
    </Step>

</Steps>

That's it! You have successfully installed WebAgent.ai onto your Squarespace website. The chatbot script should now be embedded on your website, allowing you to engage with your site visitors intelligently.
