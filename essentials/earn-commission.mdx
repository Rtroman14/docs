<Note>This feature is only available for users subscribed to the Business or Enterprise plan.</Note>

Earn a 20% commission forever by promoting WebAgent.ai through our affiliate program. Simply update the **Powered By** link on your chatbot with your unique affiliate link, and earn commissions whenever someone clicks and subscribes to WebAgent.ai.

<Steps titleSize="h2">
    <Step title="Sign up as an Affiliate">
        <ol>
            <li>
                Go to our affiliate program [sign-up page](https://webagent.tolt.io/login) and
                register as an affiliate.
            </li>
        </ol>

        <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/images/webagent-affiliate.png" />
    </Step>
    <Step title="Update the 'Powered By' Link">
        <ol>
            <li>Log in to your WebAgent.ai account and go to the **Appearance** page.</li>
            <li>Scroll down and locate the **Powered By** input.</li>
            <li>Replace the href attribute with your affiliate link.</li>
        </ol>

        <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="/images/webagent-powered-by-affiliate.png" />
    </Step>
    <Step title="Earn 20% Commission">
        <ol>
            <li>
                For every user who clicks on the **Powered By** link and subscribes to WebAgent.ai,
                you will earn a 20% commission forever.
            </li>
        </ol>
    </Step>

</Steps>
